How it works

From idea to alarm in seconds

Alarm & Reminder is designed to feel like a tiny mobile alarm app — but lives right inside your browser toolbar.

1. Click and add

Click the extension icon, tap the + button, scroll the hours and minutes, give your alarm a label and choose an emoji icon.

2. Pick sound, snooze & repeat

Choose from built‑in sounds, set how long snooze should last and pick which days of the week the alarm repeats.

3. Let Chrome do the rest

When it’s time, a large ringing view (optionally in its own window) shows big Snooze and Stop buttons so you can react instantly — even if Chrome was in the background.
