Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how "Alarm & Reminder" (the "Extension") handles your information. Our primary goal is to provide a powerful utility while collecting as little data as possible.

2. Data Collection & Storage

The Extension is designed to operate locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal identification information (PII) to use the core features of the Extension.

  • Alarm Data: All your alarm settings, labels, and schedules are stored locally in your browser's storage (Chrome Storage API). This data is never sent to our servers.
  • Custom Audio: If you upload custom MP3 files (Premium feature), these are stored locally within your browser profile.
  • Voice Messages: TTS (Text-to-Speech) messages are processed by your browser's native API. No text data is recorded or transmitted by us.

3. Security of Your Data

Since your data lives on your own device, its security depends on the security of your computer and browser. We do not have access to your alarms, labels, or browsing history.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Lemon Squeezy as our Merchant of Record for Premium license processing. When you purchase a Premium license:

  • Your payment information (credit card details, address) is collected and processed securely by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store or see your full payment details.
  • We receive your email address and a license key from Lemon Squeezy to verify your Premium status.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
