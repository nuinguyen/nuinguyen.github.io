Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a video to my playlist?

Simply click the "Save to Playlist" button that appears below the YouTube video player after installing the extension.

Does the sleep timer work for music?

Yes! The sleep timer will pause any active YouTube video or music track once the time runs out.

Can I customize my playlist icons?

Absolutely. You can choose from a variety of colors and icons to make each playlist unique and easy to find.
